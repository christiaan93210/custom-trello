Frequently Asked Questions

What is the vision for the D&IT Lab of Excellence?

The D&IT Lab of Excellence envisions being the leading hub for innovation and technology in the region, where it facilitates the collaboration between industry leaders, academic institutions, startups. The center aims to empower businesses to tackle complex challenges through cutting-edge technologies, such as AI, blockchain, and AR/VR, and to foster a culture of continuous learning and development. By providing an environment that fosters creativity, experimentation, and learning, the center aims to help organizations transform and thrive in the digital age.

What services does the center offer?

The center provides several services such as:

Idea incubation and acceleration programs.
Collaboration sessions and brainstorming.
Access to advanced technology and equipment, including a modular datacenter and user experience lab.
Mentorship and guidance from industry experts.
Networking opportunities with mentors and peers.
Training and workshops on various topics, including innovation and entrepreneurship.
Access to funding opportunities.
Events and conferences on innovation and technology trends.
Co-working and office space for startups .

How can I reserve an Incubator?

If you're looking to reserve an incubator, you can start by submitting your idea through our online form. Our specialized team will carefully review your request and assess whether it aligns with our goals and values. If your idea is accepted, we'll provide you with an incubator to help bring your idea to life. Our incubators are equipped with all the necessary resources and support to help you succeed. So, don't hesitate to submit your idea and take the first step towards realizing your innovative vision.

How can I be part of the D&IT Lab of Excellence Team?

We appreciate your interest in joining the D&IT Lab of Excellence team. If you are interested in exploring career opportunities with us, we invite you to send us your CV through our email address provided on our website. Our recruitment team will review your application and will contact you if your skills and experience match any current or future opportunities. We appreciate your time and look forward to hearing from you soon.

Can I use the center if I don't have a business idea yet? 

Yes, the center welcome everyone. You can always book our facility to collaboration sessions and meeting with clients, partners, stakeholders, and experts. Our team will gladly assist you to brainstorm addressing business challenges and identifying solutions. 
You can also utilize our facility if you're interested in conducting webinars, digital talks, or panel discussions.

What distinguishes the center from others?

The center stands out from other innovation centers in several ways. Firstly, our state-of-the-art modular datacenter provides a secure and isolated environment for users to experiment and develop their ideas without risking interference with the corporate system. Additionally, our computing resources are assigned instantly, allowing users to work more efficiently and quickly.

Moreover, we have a user experience lab that enables our users to test their solutions and receive feedback from experts to refine their products before deploying them to the market. Our lab features the latest equipment and technologies to ensure the best user experience for our clients' products.

In addition to our unique infrastructure, the center provides an extensive range of services and resources to support our users throughout their innovation journey. Our team of experts provides guidance, mentorship, and training programs to help our users turn their ideas into successful businesses. Furthermore, we offer a variety of networking events, workshops, and webinars that bring together innovators, entrepreneurs, and investors to exchange ideas and collaborate.
